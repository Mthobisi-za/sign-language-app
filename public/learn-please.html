Please

In this lesson I am going to teach you how to say "Please"

The sign for “please” is made by first placing your dominant hand with your thumb sticking out and fingers extended over your chest. Secondly, move your hand in circular motion (clockwise) two or three times. It is often used at the end of a sentence if he/she wants something.
